About

Apiary Coding is a collaborative online and physical learning space for women and non-binary folk of all knowledge levels to learn tech skills together.

We don't have any tutors or syllabus - our aim is to help each other learn what we want to learn, how we want to learn.

Safer Spaces Policy

Apiary Coding is committed to all atendees at our events having a safe and enjoyable experience and, as such, operates under a Safer Spaces Policy.

By attending an event run by Apiary Coding, you agree to the following:

  • Apiary Coding events are open only to those who identify as a woman or non-binary gender/genders.
  • However, how someone identifies isn't open for debate - harassment based on someone's percieved gender will not be tolerated.
  • Indeed, Apiary Coding has zero tolerance for harassment - that is, behaviour that may make someone feel distressed, humiliated or threatened - of any kind, for any reason. This applies at all spaces run by or associated with Apiary Coding (both physical and online), as well as spaces outside our control.
  • Apiary Coding expects atendees at its events to behave in a polite, courteous manner and respect the wishes of other atendees.
  • Anyone found to be violating the Safer Spaces Policy will be removed and banned from Apiary Coding spaces indefinitely.
