About

Apiary Coding is a collaborative online and physical learning space for women and non-binary folk of all knowledge levels to learn tech skills together.

We don't have any tutors or syllabus - we aim to provide a friendly, supportive community and help each other find the best resources to learn what we want to learn, how we want to learn it.

How it Works

Apiary Coding is open to everyone who identifies as a woman or non-binary gender(s) and wants to improve their tech skills.

You don't have to be a part of or want to join the tech industry. We believe everyone can benefit from learning a bit more about tech, whatever their aims, abilities or existing knowledge.

Online

Check out our list of free learning resources from across the web.

Join our Slack group! It's invitation-only, but invites are freely granted. Just email apiarycoding@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

In our Slack, you can chat and ask questions with fellow Apiary Coding learners. Just find a channel related to what you want to learn - or create one if it doesn't exist yet - and get talking!

At a Meet Up

We hold regular meet ups in central Edinburgh - you can find out when and where our next meet up will be below. Meet ups are open to all women/non-binary folk who'd like to attend - just bring along something you can learn on (preferably a laptop).

Everyone at an Apiary Coding meet up wears a sticker with what they want to learn that day and what they can help others with

If you see someone with a similar skill on their sticker, say "Hi!"

And learn together!
